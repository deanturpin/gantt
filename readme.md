Simple command line Gantt chart.

```bash
./gantt.sh < tasks.txt 
|##### 3. Sequi esse et occaecati perferendis
|     ########## 4. Rerum laboriosam itaque cupiditate
|               # 5. Commodi dolorem quas debitis
|                ## 6. Voluptas at repudiandae qui
|                  #### 7. Earum nesciunt
|                      ####### 8. Possimus non et
|                             ######## 9. Amet
|                                     ######### 10. Amet sit
|                                              ## 11. Reiciendis
|                                                ######## 12. Qui
|______________________________________________________________________

Tasks 12
Weeks 11
Start 01 Feb 2017
Compl 20 Apr 2017
```

The tasks are stored in a plain text file: title followed by a space and a
duration in whole days.
```
Sequi esse et occaecati perferendis 5
Rerum laboriosam itaque cupiditate 10
Commodi dolorem quas debitis 1
Voluptas at repudiandae qui 2
Earum nesciunt 4
Possimus non et 7
Amet 8
Amet sit 9
Reiciendis 2
Qui 8
```
